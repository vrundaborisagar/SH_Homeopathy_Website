Cervico-Brachial Neuralgia

Cervico-Brachial Neuralgia is caused by irritation or compression of nerves in the neck, radiating pain into the arms. It can be triggered by disc issues, poor posture, trauma, or repetitive strain.

Symptoms

  • Sharp/aching pain from neck to arm and fingers
  • Tingling or pins-and-needles in the hand
  • Muscle weakness in the arm or hand
  • Numbness along the nerve path
  • Burning sensations and stiffness

What Makes It Worse

  • Poor posture or prolonged neck bending
  • Heavy lifting or jerky movements
  • Cold drafts and lack of rest

Precautions

  • Maintain proper posture while sitting/sleeping
  • Avoid lifting heavy objects
  • Practice gentle neck stretches
